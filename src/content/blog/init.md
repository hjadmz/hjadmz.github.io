Every system needs an origin point. This is it.

Not a manifesto. A marker. The log starts here and accumulates forward.

## What happens here

Process documentation. Design rationale. Engineering decisions that don't fit in a commit message.

## What doesn't

Listicles. Tutorials rewritten from the docs. Content for content's sake.

The work is the point.
