## Premise

Most personal sites lead with aesthetics and hope the substance trails behind. This one inverts the order: research and function come first, convenience second, style last — the FCA protocol. The result is a system where every visible decision is load-bearing.

## System, not theme

The palette is strictly monochrome — `#020617` on `#F9FAFB` — verified against WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA at every interactive state. Typography is Inter (UI) and JetBrains Mono (technical), scaled on a Major Third ratio. Motion rides a single critically overdamped spring (mass 2, stiffness 300, damping 49) so every transition settles with zero bounce.

## Accessibility as substrate

Respect for `prefers-reduced-motion`, `prefers-color-scheme`, and `hover: hover` is baked in — not bolted on. Focus rings inherit border radius. Tap highlights are disabled in favor of explicit feedback. Inputs use 16px on mobile to prevent iOS zoom-on-focus.

## Authoring

Content lives in MDX with a typed Zod schema. Adding a log entry or a work item is one file. The command palette, search, and sitemap wire up automatically. The developer experience is the product: the system has to be boring enough to use every day.
